Masala rice


Masala rice is a flavoured and spicy rice recipe prepared with dry spices and vegetables. it is a one-pot meal and hence an ideal lunch box recipe and easily appreciated by kids and all age groups. the rice is infused with spices and masala with choice of veggies and hence served by itself or tastes great with raita.

Ingredients needed:

  • 1 tsp of mustard, cumin
  • 1/2 tsp fennel
  • Pinch of hing
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • few curry leaves and 1 green chilli
  • 1/2 onion, 1 tomato, 1/2 carrot, 2tsp green peas
  • 1/4 turmeric, 3/4 red chilli powder, 1/2 garam masala
  • 2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp corriander

Steps:

  1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 1 tbsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp fennel, pinch hing and few curry leaves.
  2. Now add ½ tsp ginger garlic paste, 1 green chilli and ½ onion. Saute well.
  3. Additionally, add 1 tomato and saute till tomatoes turn soft & mushy.
  4. Further add chopped vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Now add ¼ tsp turmeric, ¾ tsp red chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder and ½ tsp garam masala.
  6. Saute until the spices start to release oil.
  7. Furthermore, add 2 cups cooked rice and 1 tsp salt.
  8. Mix gently without breaking rice grains.
  9. Cover and simmer for 4 minutes.
  10. Finally, add coriander leaves and serve masala rice with raita.
